# Acceptable use

cherrybomb is built for **ethical** research, journalism, security work and data
science. By using or contributing to cherrybomb you agree to the following.

## Permitted

- Collecting **public** data for research, journalism, academic study, or security
  analysis you are authorized to perform.
- Analyzing your own accounts/communities, or data you have explicit permission to
  collect.

## Forbidden

- Harassment, stalking, doxxing, blackmail, threats, or intimidation of any person.
- Surveillance of individuals without lawful basis.
- Building profiles to enable discrimination or targeting of protected groups.
- Any use that violates applicable law in your jurisdiction.

## Your responsibility

cherrybomb is a tool. Scraping login-walled platforms commonly violates those
platforms' Terms of Service and may breach local law. **You** are solely responsible
for ensuring your use is lawful and ethical. The in-app disclaimer and this document
are informed-consent notices, **not** legal authorization or a shield.

Maintainers may remove any platform adapter at any time in response to legal demands.
